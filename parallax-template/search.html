Project Bio

We are a team of DU Coding Bootcamp students working on this project. Our goal is to make it easy for someone to search the availability for a specific audio book or e-book, across different digital library collections, throughout the greater Denver area. Any amount of help and support to continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated.
