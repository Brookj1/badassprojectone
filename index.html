Project Background

This project began with the need for a more seamless way to search for borrowable e-books and e-audiobooks across multiple library collections and digital resouce subscriptions. The desired outcome is a one-stop solution to view freely accessible e-book and e-audiobook availability throughout specific regional libraries and openly available collections.

A modern search tool for digital collections.

About us

We are a team of University of Denver Coding Bootcamp students working on this project. Our goal is to make digitized materials in libraries and open collections more accessible. Any amount of help and support to continue development on this project and is greatly appreciated. Please see our personal links on the right for more information about us.
